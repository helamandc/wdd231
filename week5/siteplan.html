Site Name

Melbourne Peace in Christ | Religious Community Website

Site Purpose

Sharing messages of peace in Victoria and Tasmania. I have found a new friend at church whom the full-time missionaries are teaching. That friend invited me to baptize him. Since then, we have been together in his new member lessons. I really feel good about working with the full-time missionaries. It makes me happy. This website will be an extension to their facebook page of the same name which will enable someone to browse through an actual church community in Melbourne.

Scenarios

  • You can watch videos from local members and missionaries in Melbourne sharing their testimonies about Jesus Christ.
  • You can check upcoming activities and schedule of Sunday church services.
  • You can register so local missionaries can reach out to you and share a message of peace.
